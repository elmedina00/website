Albanian Alps

The Albanian Alps, also known as the Accursed Mountains, are a stunning mountain range in northern Albania, famous for their rugged landscapes, deep valleys, and towering peaks. This pristine natural paradise offers breathtaking views, picturesque villages, and a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, with opportunities for hiking, trekking, and exploring ancient trails.
